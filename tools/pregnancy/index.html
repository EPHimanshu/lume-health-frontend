      Frequently asked questions

      How is pregnancy due date calculated?

      A due date is usually estimated from the first day of your last period, conception date, IVF transfer date, ultrasound details, or an already known due date.

      What if my periods are irregular?

      If your cycle is not the standard 28 days, adding your average cycle length may help. Your doctor may also use ultrasound dating for a more accurate estimate.

      Is the due date always exact?

      No. A due date is an estimate. Many babies are born before or after the estimated date.

      Can I calculate using IVF transfer date?

      Yes. This page includes a separate IVF option with embryo age at transfer.

      Can I use ultrasound details?

      Yes. If you know the ultrasound date and the pregnancy week and days shown on that date, you can use the ultrasound option.
